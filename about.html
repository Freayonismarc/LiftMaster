About Our Company

Was were established  on May 11, 2004. We are a  direct importer and specialist of forklift spare parts & accessories, hydraulic hoses, tires, and seal kits for all brands of Forklift units :TCM, Komatsu,Mitsubishi, Toyota, Yale, Daewoo, Caterpillar, Hyster , Doosan and others.  We also import used Forklifts from Japan. Presently, we are an authorized dealerof Brand new HELI Forklifts in the Philippines. At present, we have 2 branches and 6 subsidiary branches nationwide. We are a subsidiary of SIMPLEX INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION, a leading supplier of sealing products in the Philippines with more than 34 years in service to the manufacturing companies (Foods and Non Foods),     construction companies, mining companies, logistics companies and others.
